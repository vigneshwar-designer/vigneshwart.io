Selected Projects

Real problems, thoughtful solutions, measurable results.

01

AUREXON

Branding & Identity

Problem

Many modern skincare brands lack clarity in positioning, often appearing either too clinical or overly aesthetic without communicating real value. This creates confusion and reduces trust among conscious consumers looking for clean and minimal skincare solutions.

Solution

Developed a calm and minimal brand identity centered on clean, conscious skincare. Designed a refined serif-based logo to reflect elegance and trust, supported by a nature-inspired color palette (forest green, sage, beige, botanical gold). Created a cohesive visual system including packaging, typography, and brand communication that emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and modern living.

Tools Used

Illustrator Photoshop Canva
Expected Outcome — A visually consistent and trustworthy brand identity that enhances product perception and builds stronger emotional connection with conscious consumers.
02

RAWSTATE

Branding & Identity

Problem

Many streetwear brands lack a strong identity and end up looking repetitive, with no clear personality or emotional connection. This makes it difficult to stand out in a saturated and trend-driven market.

Solution

Created a bold and raw brand identity focused on simplicity and attitude. Designed a strong typographic logo with a high-contrast black, white, and red palette to reflect energy and street culture. Applied the identity across apparel mockups, tags, and brand visuals to maintain a consistent and impactful presence.

Tools Used

Illustrator Photoshop
Design Impact — A distinctive and recognizable visual identity that strengthens brand attitude and improves audience connection in the streetwear space.
03

LOGIQO

Branding & UI Identity

Problem

Many productivity and SaaS brands struggle with cluttered interfaces and inconsistent branding, which reduces usability and makes the product feel less reliable and harder to trust.

Solution

Designed a clean and structured brand identity focused on logic, clarity, and usability. Developed a minimal logo with a balanced typography system and a calm blue-toned palette to represent intelligence and focus. Extended the identity into UI design, creating a simple and user-friendly dashboard and mobile interface that improves clarity and workflow.

Tools Used

Illustrator Photoshop Figma
Expected Outcome — A clear and structured brand experience that enhances usability, improves user trust, and supports better productivity.
